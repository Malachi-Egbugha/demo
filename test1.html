OUR VALUES

Some of Our values are independent thinking, diversity, respect, accountability and responsibility. These values are infused into everyday life at the school.

Our shared values are shared by all staff, teachers and students. They permeate in lessons from teachers right down to how our students treat one another. It is these very values that separate Danbolites from the rest.

Our values are reflected in all of us through our actions. They have become an implicit part of our lifes and what makes us who we are as people. We chose to represent our values by their simplest and most powerful actions, with five images to best convey their sentiments.

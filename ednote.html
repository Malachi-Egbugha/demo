Founder's note

Danbo International School was founded due to personal necessity; a necessity formed out of love. In 1981, it was a challenge to find quality education for my children, and as a parent, nothing drives one more than the best we wish for our children. On this personal journey, I found my calling and passion, the quest to provide all children with the best, the best that would equip and prepare them for right skills and experiences for a successful future.

Education forms the basis of the school’s mantra, high achievement for all. Providing a holistic education where children evolve as individuals who are self-motivated and creative, who can think, question and reason out logically, who are independent, confident and leaders capable of taking decisions. I believe Education is a complete process that leads to the attainment of full potential of every child and leaving no one behind.

In the last 39 years, DIS has grown – from a 15 student class with 6 teachers – to a school with a capacity of over 1,200 students, employing hundreds of teachers and support staff, with modern facilities. We now have a new cosy branch located in Jabi Abuja and excited about the prospects this new branch holds.

Over the years, we are fortunate to have had a great team of dedicated educators, and a reliable team of operations and administrative staff providing the most valuable support. I am most grateful to all the parents who have entrusted their children’s Education to DIS.

As I said Danbo International School started from personal necessity and is now well into its mission and commitment to providing top-class Education to the students from all walks of life.

I ascribe to the old saying that “Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today. – Malcolm X” We have a long way to go, so let us support each other in nurturing our next generation for a better future.

Mrs Bosede Yusuf Bello
Executive Director
